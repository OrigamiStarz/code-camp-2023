About

Jr. CODE camp is an annual camp that lasts two weeks, typically occuring in late May - early June of each year. It is led by members of Glen A. Wilson High School's CODE team, who design a fun and exciting curriculum to teach your kids the basics of web development—a fundamental skill in coding. Throughout this camp, your kids will explore web development through the applications of HTML, CSS and JavaScript. By the end of the camp, your kids will be able to individually design and develop a website with a basic structure and functions. We promise to provide your kids the best experience possible!

FAQ

What will my child get from attending?

Campers will learn the basics of web development.

Does my child need any prior coding experience?

Nope. All experience levels are welcome!

Do I need to bring anything?

Just water and a school laptop. You can also bring snacks to eat during break.

Who are welcome to join?

This camp is open to incoming 3rd to 5th graders.
